Rules of Baseball Sports

  1. Player Eligibility: Players must meet eligibility requirements set by the league, including age restrictions and contractual obligations.
  2. Game Format: Baseball games consist of nine innings. Teams take turns batting and fielding. The team with the most runs at the end of the game wins.
  3. Team Composition: Teams consist of nine players, including a pitcher, catcher, infielders, and outfielders.
  4. Gameplay: Players must follow the rules of baseball, including the strike zone, base running, and defensive strategies.
  5. Umpire Decisions: Umpires enforce the rules and make decisions during the game, including calling balls and strikes, and safe or out calls.
